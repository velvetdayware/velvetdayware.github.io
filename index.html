The Fabrics

Why fabric choice changes everything

When you're wearing the same dress from 7am to 7pm, what it's made of matters as much as how it looks.

100% Cotton

Natural, breathable, and soft against skin. Cotton washes well, holds its shape, and gets better with every wear. Our cotton dresses are pre-washed for minimal shrinkage.

Linen Blend

The texture of linen with added softness from the blend. Naturally temperature-regulating, which means it works in warm weather and layers well when it's cooler.

Soft Jersey

Stretchy, forgiving, and effortlessly draped. Jersey moves with your body without clinging. It's the fabric that makes getting dressed feel like putting on a comfortable sweater.
