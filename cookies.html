Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 2 March 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Velvet Dayware ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on velvetdayware.com. By using our website, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit, such as your preferences and actions on the site.

Cookies may be "session cookies" (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (stored on your device for a set period or until you delete them).

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as shopping cart operation, page navigation, and access to secure areas. The website cannot function correctly without these cookies, and they cannot be disabled.

Examples include: session management cookies, cart and checkout cookies, cookie consent preference cookies.

Analytics Cookies (Optional)

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve the structure, content, and functionality of our site.

Examples include: page view tracking, session duration, navigation paths, and device type information. These cookies do not identify you personally.

Marketing Cookies (Optional)

Marketing cookies are used to deliver content that is more relevant to you and your interests. They may be set by us or by third-party advertising partners. These cookies track your browsing activity across websites to build a profile of your interests.

You can opt out of marketing cookies through our cookie consent tool or through your browser settings.

3. How to Manage Cookies

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can choose to accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or customize your preferences by category.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your cookies and revisiting the site, which will prompt the consent banner to reappear.

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website, including the shopping cart and checkout process.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third parties, such as analytics providers or advertising networks. These third parties have their own privacy policies governing their use of cookies. We do not control third-party cookies and are not responsible for their content or practices.

5. Cookie Duration

Essential cookies: Duration varies; some are session cookies that expire when you close your browser. Others persist for up to 12 months to remember your preferences.

Analytics cookies: Typically persist for 12–24 months.

Marketing cookies: Typically persist for 30–90 days.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, law, or our practices. Updated policies will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
