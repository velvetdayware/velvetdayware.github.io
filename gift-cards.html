Why check your gift card balance?

Knowing your remaining gift card balance before shopping helps you plan your purchase and avoid declined transactions at checkout. Whether you received a card as a gift or purchased one yourself, always verify before you shop.

Gift cards can lose value over time due to inactivity fees, or you may have forgotten how much you've spent. This tool gives you a quick way to verify before heading to the store or placing an order online.

Tips for using gift cards

  • Always check your balance before a large purchase to avoid a split-tender transaction.
  • Keep your physical card and receipt until the balance reaches zero.
  • For digital gift cards, save your code in a secure notes app.
  • Some brands allow you to combine multiple gift card balances at checkout.
  • Check expiry and inactivity fee policies on the brand's official website.
  • Register your gift card with the brand for loss protection where available.
