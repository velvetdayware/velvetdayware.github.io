Velvet Dayware Gift Cards

When you want to give someone something she'll actually use — and let her choose exactly what suits her life.

The gift of choosing for herself

Sometimes the most thoughtful gift is the one that doesn't require you to guess someone's size, color preference, or current wardrobe needs. A Velvet Dayware gift card gives her the freedom to browse the collection and pick exactly what feels right for her day-to-day life.

Gift cards are applied at checkout — in full or in part. If the order total exceeds the card value, the remaining balance can be paid by another method. If the card covers the full order, no additional payment is needed.

To inquire about purchasing a Velvet Dayware gift card, contact us by email or phone and we'll walk you through the options.

How gift cards work

Request a Card

Contact us to request a gift card. We'll confirm the denomination and delivery method with you.

Give It

Pass along the gift card code to the recipient — digitally or as part of a physical gift.

She Shops

She browses the collection, adds items to her bag, and proceeds to checkout when ready.

Code Applied

At checkout, she enters the gift card code and clicks Apply. The discount is reflected immediately in her order total.

Gift Card FAQs

Do gift cards expire?

Velvet Dayware gift cards do not have an expiration date. The recipient can use the card whenever she's ready.

Can a gift card be used for a partial payment?

Yes. If the order total exceeds the gift card balance, the remaining amount can be covered by another payment method at checkout.

Are gift cards refundable?

Gift cards are non-refundable but are transferable — the recipient can pass the code along to someone else if she chooses.

What denominations are available?

Contact us to discuss available denominations. We'll work with you to find an amount that fits the occasion.

How do I give the gift card to someone?

Once your gift card is processed, we'll provide a unique code you can share digitally or include in a physical card.
